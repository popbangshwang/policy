Privacy Policy for Swipe2Shop

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Swipe2Shop mobile application.

Information We Collect

Swipe2Shop collects the following data:

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage information through Firebase Analytics to help us understand how users interact with our app. This includes information like app features used, time spent in the app, and actions taken.
  • Crash Reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports that help us identify and fix technical issues. These reports include information about your device, operating system version, and the state of the app when it crashed.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To improve the app's functionality and user experience
  • To identify and fix technical issues
  • To understand which features are most valuable to our users

User Controls

We respect your privacy and provide controls for data collection:

  • You can disable Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics data collection in the app settings
  • When these features are disabled, no usage or crash data will be collected

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. The anonymous data collected through Firebase services is processed according to Google's Firebase Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Shopping List Data

All your shopping list data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your shopping lists, items, or tags unless you choose to share them.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the app.
