Privacy Policy for Swipe2Shop

This privacy policy explains how Swipe2Shop mobile application handles your information.

Information We Collect

Swipe2Shop does not collect any personal data or usage information. The app functions entirely offline and does not transmit any data to external servers.

Your Shopping List Data

All your shopping list data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your shopping lists, items, or tags. Your data remains private and never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it using your device's sharing functionality.

Device Permissions

Swipe2Shop may request certain permissions to provide functionality, such as storage access to save your shopping list data. These permissions are used only for app functionality and not for data collection.

Third-Party Services

Swipe2Shop does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services. We do not share any information with third parties.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security is managed by your device's built-in security features. We recommend keeping your device's operating system updated to maintain security.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not collect personal information from any users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the app.
